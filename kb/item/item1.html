Austin Community Family Center Website

This was a project in which I and several other DePaul CDM students worked with a Chicago community to build a website. I worked as the liaison between the Austin Community Center and our group. My contributions include working on Axure prototype, interviewing and communicating with clients, and assisting in the management process by outlining the project scope and distributing work among groupmembers.

Project Details

  • Working with Chicago clients
  • Axure Prototyping
  • Wordpress site development
  • Instruction documentation & Portfolio for client use
