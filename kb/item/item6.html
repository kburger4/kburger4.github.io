Project Description

These pieces were created utilizing Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. The first piece on the left was an immitation piece of David Carson. Carson's work notoriously rejects traditional graphic design principles, though there is a method to the madness. His work isn't intended to look like a mess, rather an interesting piece from afar that requires closer inspection. This piece attempted to mimic his style - it is a poster for Moogfest 2015. Prior to making this piece, I did research on Carson's work and his life. You can find related pieces below and some of my pattern work below.
